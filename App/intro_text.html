Data Anomaly Detection Tool of MER Data

Welcome to an interactive tool for the detection of anomalous observations in PEPFAR Monitoring, Evaluation, and Reporting (MER) data. 

What can you do with this tool:

  • Detect anomalies algorithmically using Recommender Models. This approach identifies patterns across MER indicators and flags those observations whose reported data is unlikely given globally observed patterns.

  • Detect anomalies in reported MER data using Time Series models, accounting for historical trends and seasonal patterns.

  • Use recommender systems to analyze one quarter at a time. For reliable results, the recommender system should be used with a minimum of ten facilities.

  • Use time series models on any number of facilities. Data going back 12 quarters is necessary.

  • Explore results and compare by Implementing Partner, Facility, and Indicator.

  • Download results for easier distribution, use, and documentation.

For a brief tutorial of the settings and tools click the instructions button at the top of the sidebar.
