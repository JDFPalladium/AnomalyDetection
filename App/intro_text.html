Data.FI Anomaly Detection Tool

Welcome to an interactive tool for the detection of anomalous observations in MER data. 

What can you do with this tool:

  • Detect anomalies algorithmically using Recommender Models, an approach that identifies patterns across MER indicators and flags those observations whose reported data is unlikely given globally observed patterns.

  • Detect anomalies using Time Series models, accounting for historical trends and seasonal patterns.

  • Explore results and compare by Implementing Partner, Facility, and Indicator.

  • Download results for easier distribution.

For a brief tutorial of the settings and tools click the instructions button at the top of the sidebar.
